GUIDELINES

  • Only one team per department is allowed.
  • Maximum 12 members per team can participate.
  • Both UG and PG students are allowed.
  • Identity card of the student is mandatory.
  • Participants should register only through online mode, on spot registration is not encouraged.
  • Registration fee Rs.150 per participant should be paid on or before 12th March 2023
  • A participant can participant in any two technical and two non technical events only.
  • It is a team's responsibility to make sure that no events clash with one another.
  • Food and Refreshments will be provided
  • Decisions of the jury is final.
